# Decision 0002 - AI-first Platform Principles

## Decision

The platform follows these principles:

1. AI is the primary builder
2. AI may also be the primary operator
3. Humans provide intent and supervision
4. All primitives must be machine-readable
5. Errors must be machine-actionable
6. State must be platform-owned
7. Permissions enforced centrally
8. Execution must be guarded
9. Custom logic must be sandboxed

---

## Why

Traditional systems assume:

> humans operate software

This system assumes:

> AI operates software
